Qualtric IAQ Survey — Symbol Design Options

Pick a symbol design for the Qualtric IAQ Survey layer. The base shape is a diamond (so it's never confused with a community-contact circle). For G3 (Qualtric-only / flyer / QR — no community contact at this parcel) we add a purple outline plus a distinctive glyph. For G1 (matched — community contact + Qualtric) the diamond renders behind the green contact circle, so the corners peek out as the "this contact also has IAQ" cue.

Diamond fill = IAQ risk tier (green = low, amber = medium, red = high)
Purple outline = G3 (Qualtric only, no community contact)
Diamond corners + green circle = G1 (matched contact + Qualtric)
