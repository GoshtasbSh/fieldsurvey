Invite a Member by Email
Enter a candidate's email; we email them a one-time invitation link. They sign up with that exact email + their own password, and are auto-promoted to member. The link expires in 14 days and self-destructs after first use. This is the only public path to a member account — random visitors cannot self-register.

Daily Change-Report Recipients
Each day at 8 PM Eastern, if the dashboard data changed (community contacts, field points, or IAQ surveys added/edited) we send these recipients two Excel attachments: a unified community-and-field export, and the IAQ survey export. No email is sent on days with no changes. Add as many addresses as you like; tick a row to deactivate it without deleting.
